How we calculate your annual salary?

Yearly income is determined by multiplying the hourly rate by the number of hours worked weekly and then by the total weeks worked annually.

As a reference, a typical full-time workweek consists of approximately 40 hours. Should you opt for two weeks of unpaid vacation annually, the calculation should be based on 50 weeks instead of the standard 52.

Applying this method allows for the computation of annual earnings starting from an hourly wage. Bear in mind, these calculations provide gross income figures, before taxes and any other deductions are applied.
